Know About Us

The Indian government formed this organization in April 1995 by merging two organizations: One, International Airports Authority of India (IAAI) that was founded in 1972 to manage the nation's international airports and two, the National Airports Authority (NAA) that was formed in 1986 to look after domestic airports.

Facts & Figures

  • 10 consecutive years of Profitable operations
  • Market share of 54.3% as of January, 2021.
  • Fleet of 281 aircraft including 120 new generation A320 NEOs, 102 A320 CEOs, 25 ATRs and 34 A321 NEO.
  • Recognized as ‘Great Place to Work for in India’ for 8 years in a row (2008- 2015)
  • Named as Aon’s Best Employer for the year 2016 and 2017
